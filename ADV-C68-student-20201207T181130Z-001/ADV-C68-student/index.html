The Khaitan School

Hi!

Founded in April 1995, The Khaitan School is a leading CBSE school in Noida. Sprawled over 6.35 acres, its vibrant ambience, sporting facilities, progressive infrastructure and adoption of experiential learning, provide a stimulating environment for the all-round growth and development of children. Our international school programs and engagements have helped children connect with peers from various countries such as USA, France, Japan, Korea and Germany, ensuring a truly global school experience along with an understanding and sensitisation of local issues. Education World Magazine and Times of India survey's have consistently ranked us amongst the top schools of Noida. In the past 25 years, over 1400 students have graduated from The Khaitan School who are now part of a rich alumni comprising doctors, lawyers, engineers, artists, fashion designers, businessman, architects, amongst many other careers and have been placed in the top colleges and universities in India and abroad, such as BITS Pilani, Imperial College London, Vellore Institute of Technology, University of Hong Kong, Kirori Mal College, DU, Manipal, Mangalore., to name a few..
